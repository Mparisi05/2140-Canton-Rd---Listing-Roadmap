Your Property Marketing Plan

30-Day Listing Roadmap

A strategic approach to bringing your property to market in a timely manner

Week 1 December 15 – 21

Kickoff, discovery, and initial outreach

Monday December 15
  • Execute listing agreement
  • Site visit: measure ceiling heights and drive-in bay dimensions
    • Document building features: wall system, number of bays, electrical capacity/system, HVAC size/brand, fire safety systems, utility providers
  • Review quotes for pressure washing and general cleanup ideas
  • Schedule floorplan drawing and virtual walkthrough content for Friday
  • Schedule photography for Friday (depending on property prep status)
Tuesday December 16
  • Meet with marketing director to kick off design process for custom marketing materials
  • Draft email template for responding to inquiries (based on site visit notes)
  • Draft email to brokerage community announcing upcoming listing
  • Prepare email campaigns for Wednesday morning
  • Schedule photographers, videographer, architects, and sign company
Wednesday December 17
  • Email campaign to 1,000+ local contractors announcing upcoming listing
  • Send email to 900+ local commercial real estate brokers — capture hot leads before the holiday season
  • Deep research on all similar Canton Rd properties to refine sales pitch and competitive positioning
Thursday December 18
  • Reach out to existing buyer — update on professional listing status and marketing timeline to create pressure
Friday December 19
  • Floorplan drawing and virtual walkthrough content performed
  • Photography and videography taken (weather permitting, depending on property prep status)
Saturday December 20
  • Backup day for photography if needed
Week 2 December 22 – 28 (Christmas Week)

Finalize research and prepare campaigns

Monday December 22
  • Finalize deep research on Canton Rd property comparison
  • Draft email campaign for farm & garden supply stores (approved zoning use)
  • Draft email campaign for exterminators (approved zoning use)
Tue – Sun December 23 – 28

Christmas week — no activity scheduled

Week 3 December 29 – January 4 (New Year's Week)

Deploy targeted email campaigns

Monday December 29
  • Send email campaign to farm & garden supply store prospects
  • Send email campaign to exterminator prospects
  • Revisit photography scheduling if not yet completed
Tue – Sat December 30 – January 4

New Year's week — no activity scheduled

Week 4 January 5 – 11

Marketing materials development and refinement

Monday January 6
  • Receive first draft of marketing materials (photography dependent)
Throughout Week January 6 – 10
  • Review and edit marketing materials
Week 5 January 12 – 18 (Launch Week)

Go live and maximize exposure

Launch Week of January 12
  • Finalize marketing materials (photography dependent)
  • Launch listing on all listing websites
  • Send targeted eblast to 1,700+ contacts actively seeking similar properties in the area
